### noting (noun, verb)
- Definition (noun): the act of making a brief written or mental record of something; a remark or observation.
- Definition (verb, present participle of "note"): observing or paying attention to something; making a written or mental record.
- Example Sentence (noun): His careful noting of the details helped solve the problem.
- Example Sentence (verb): She was noting the changes in the weather as she walked.
- Synonyms: recording, observing, remarking, noticing, registering
- Antonyms: ignoring, overlooking, disregarding
