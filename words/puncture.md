### puncture (v./n.)
- Definition: (v.) to make a small hole in something; (n.) a small hole made by a sharp object
- Example: (v.) Be careful not to puncture the tire with that nail. (n.) The bicycle had a puncture and needed to be repaired.
- Synonyms: pierce, perforate, penetrate, prick
- Antonyms: seal, close, mend, repair
