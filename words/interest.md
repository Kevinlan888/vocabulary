### interest (noun, verb)
- Definition (noun): a feeling of curiosity or concern about something; the quality that attracts attention; money paid regularly at a particular rate for the use of money lent, or for delaying the repayment of a debt.
- Definition (verb): to attract or hold the attention or curiosity of someone.
- Example Sentence (noun): She has a strong interest in art and history.
- Example Sentence (verb): The story will interest anyone who enjoys mysteries.
- Synonyms: curiosity, attention, concern, fascination, attraction
- Antonyms: indifference, boredom, disinterest, apathy
