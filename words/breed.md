### breed (v./n.)
- Definition: (v.) to produce offspring, usually by animals; (n.) a particular type or variety of animal or plant
- Example: (v.) Rabbits breed rapidly. (n.) The Labrador is a popular breed of dog.
- Synonyms: (v.) reproduce, procreate; (n.) type, variety, species
- Antonyms: (v.) destroy, eradicate
